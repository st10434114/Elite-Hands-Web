About Us

Our Story Founded in 2023 by Precious Radebe, Elite Hands emerged from a vision to transform the domestic services sector in South Africa. After witnessing the untapped potential in domestic workers and gardeners, Precious recognized the need for professional training that could elevate both skills and earning potential.

Why Choose Elite Hands?

  • Industry-Recognized Training: Our courses are designed in consultation with industry experts and employers
  • Practical Skills Focus: Hands-on training that prepares you for real-world situations
  • Flexible Learning: Choose between 6-week and 6-month courses to fit your schedule
  • Career Support: Access to job placement assistance and career guidance


    • Our Values

    Our values are rooted in the pursuit of excellence in education, empowerment through skills, and a commitment to professional growth. We believe in the power of community development and advocate for sustainable practices in all that we do. By integrating these principles, we aim to foster a holistic approach that benefits both individuals and society as a whole.
