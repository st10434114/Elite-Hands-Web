Here are some of of our General Questions

How long are your courses?

We offer flexible course durations: short-term (6 weeks) and long-term (6 months) programs to suit different learning needs and schedules.

Do I need any previous experience?

No prior experience is required for our basic courses. Advanced courses may have prerequisites which are clearly stated in the course descriptions.

How much do courses cost?

Course fees vary by program. We offer competitive pricing with special discounts for multiple course enrollments:

  • 5% off for two courses
  • 10% off for three courses
  • 15% off for four or more courses

    • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods including:

  • Bank transfers
  • Credit/Debit cards
  • Monthly payment plans
  • Cash payments at our office
